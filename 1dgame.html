Dig, fight and explore, all on a single demensional plane!

Controls:

A,D: move
O: mine
K: attack
P: place block
I: place portal

Tips:

If you stand next to lava, you can mine what is on the other side of it
Bridge gaps of lava by filling them with stone, you can also dash across if you are fast enough
Unlock the power of teleportation by bringing 20 gold back to your spawn point
The O button doubles as a way to heal when there is nothing to mine
